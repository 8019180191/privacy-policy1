Privacy Policy - SmartMenu

Last updated: March 2026

Welcome to SmartMenu, a digital restaurant menu and ordering platform. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting your personal information.

This Privacy Policy explains:

  • What data we collect and how we use it
  • How we store and protect your data
  • Your rights regarding your information
  • How to contact us

1. Data We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

When you use SmartMenu, we may collect:

  • Name
  • Phone number (for login or order updates)
  • Email address (if provided)
  • Restaurant owner details (for admin accounts)

1.2 Usage Data

  • Device information
  • Browser type and IP address
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Order activity and interactions

1.3 Location Data

  • Used to identify nearby restaurants (if enabled)
  • Improve service recommendations

1.4 Payment Information

All payments are processed securely through third-party providers. We do not store your card or UPI details.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Provide digital menu and ordering services
  • Process and manage orders
  • Improve user experience
  • Send order updates and notifications
  • Provide analytics for restaurant owners

3. Data Security

  • Secure HTTPS communication
  • Authentication systems
  • Access control for admin dashboards
  • Regular monitoring and updates

4. Your Rights

  • Access your data
  • Update or correct your information
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of notifications

5. Third-Party Services

  • Payment gateways (UPI, cards, wallets)
  • Hosting and analytics services
  • QR code and web technologies

6. Children's Privacy

SmartMenu is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page.

8. Contact Us

  • Email: support@smartmenu.com
  • Use the "Contact Us" option inside the SmartMenu platform
